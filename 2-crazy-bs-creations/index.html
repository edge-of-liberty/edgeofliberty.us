2 Crazy B's Creations

Mommy Mustache has a dream to one day run her own bakery. To bake cookies and pastries and to decorate the cutest cakes and cupcakes. Funding a bakery takes a lot of money and that is why we decided to start this shop.

Mommy Mustache was always crafty and loves to draw. When she realized she can sell her crafts and drawings as stickers, she jumped at the chance. Mommy Mustache has never been very social, so the process has been hard. So, follow us through this process of starting a small business and see us grow into the bakery we dream of.
