Come Spend Sundays with Us at The Edge of Liberty Craft Fair!

 

Every other Sunday from Mother’s Day through Halloween, enjoy a lively outdoor market featuring local artisans, fresh food, and community spirit on the edge of Liberty Township, just north of Valparaiso. Browse a vibrant mix of handmade goods, unique art, and farm-fresh products, enjoy live music and karaoke, and grab a bite from our featured food truck.

On the Sundays in between craft fairs, our farm stand is open with organically grown, seasonal produce, herbs, and edible flowers picked fresh just for you. Explore our garden walks, nature trails, fish pond, and apiary while soaking in the fresh air and good vibes.

We’re all about creating a welcoming space where everyone can connect, shop, and celebrate the simple joys of life. Whether you’re here to support local artists, stock up on fresh food, or just enjoy a beautiful Sunday outdoors, you’re part of what makes this place so special.
