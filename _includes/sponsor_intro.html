Thank you to our community sponsors. Your support helps keep vendor fees affordable, ensures our events remain free to attend, and makes it possible for small businesses, nonprofits, and independent makers to thrive.

A Craft Fair Community Sponsorship is a season-long show of support — not a sales booth. Sponsors receive a professionally printed lawn sign displayed at every Edge of Liberty Craft Fair throughout the 2026 season, placed in high-visibility areas like entrances, parking zones, and walkways.

This is community patronage, not pushy advertising. Sponsorships provide consistent local visibility while helping preserve the welcoming, family-friendly atmosphere that makes our fair special.
