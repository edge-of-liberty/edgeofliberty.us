Create Happiness House sits on one acre next to the four-acre Edge of Liberty farm, giving renters room to step outside, walk, think, and breathe.

The house property is slowly becoming a small u-pick orchard with pears, peaches, apples, strawberries, blueberries, and figs. The adjacent farm includes a community garden, market garden, pollinator garden, fish pond, and apiary.

It is quiet rural space with optional life around the edges: fresh air, seasonal events, garden walks, and places to disappear for a bit when you need a reset.
