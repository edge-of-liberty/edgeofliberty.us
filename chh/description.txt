Create Happiness House is a shared home on a working farm, designed for people who want a slower pace, a clear head, and a place that feels grounded the moment they arrive.

Tucked into the Edge of Liberty property, this is not just a place to stay — it is a place to reset. Mornings are quiet. Evenings settle in naturally. The rhythm of the farm, the open space, and the distance from noise create an environment that is hard to find and easy to appreciate.

Inside, the home offers both connection and privacy. You will have your own room, while shared spaces invite you to cook, relax, or simply exist without pressure. Upstairs brings light, views, and a welcoming kitchen and living area. Downstairs offers a different kind of comfort, with a fireplace, workout space, and direct access to the outdoors.

Outside, you are surrounded by five acres of working land, fresh air, and room to breathe. Whether you are here for a transition, a project, or simply a change of pace, the setting supports focus, calm, and clarity.

This is a place for people who appreciate a peaceful environment, take care of shared spaces, and want something more meaningful than a typical short-term stay.

Just beyond the house, the Edge of Liberty property hosts a seasonal craft fair and farm stand, bringing a steady but relaxed rhythm of activity to the land throughout the year.
