Create Happiness House offers furnished private rooms for rent in Valparaiso, Indiana, inside a quiet shared home on a five-acre farm near The Edge of Liberty.

It is built for people who need more than a bed: travel nurses, contract workers, remote workers, people in transition, and anyone who wants a calmer place to land while they figure out the next step.

## Quick Details

- Private furnished bedrooms
- Weekly and monthly options
- Fast WiFi
- On-site laundry
- Shared stocked kitchen
- Easy on-site parking pad
- Desk, TV, mini fridge, linens, towels, and daily-use basics in each room
- Quiet rural setting just north of Valparaiso
- Shared bathrooms and shared common spaces

## Who It Works Well For

- Travel nurses and healthcare workers on short assignments
- Contract workers who need furnished housing without buying household supplies
- Remote workers who want quiet, WiFi, a desk, and room to breathe
- People who prefer a respectful shared home over an apartment complex
- Anyone who values calm, routine, and a real place to recover

## The House

Your stay includes a private, well-appointed room within a thoughtfully designed shared home. Upstairs offers natural light, open views, and a welcoming kitchen and living space. Downstairs brings a more relaxed, tucked-away atmosphere with a fireplace, workout area, and direct access to the outdoors.

Step outside and you are on five acres of evolving farmland. Young fruit trees — apple, peach, fig, and asian pear — are taking root alongside established strawberry and blueberry patches, a mature mulberry, and a classic European pear.

Just beyond your door, The Edge of Liberty hosts seasonal craft fairs and farm stand days. That gives the property a gentle rhythm of optional activity: browse vendors, meet local makers, or simply enjoy the energy without getting in your car.

## What This Is Not

- Not a private apartment or standalone unit
- Not a party house
- Not walkable city living
- Not a high-traffic social environment

This is a furnished shared home for people who want peace without isolation, practical comfort without clutter, and a place that feels cared for.
