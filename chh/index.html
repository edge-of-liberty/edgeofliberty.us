Create Happiness House

Create Happiness House is a shared home on a working farm at the Edge of Liberty — designed for people who need a reset, not just a place to stay.

This is not a typical short-term rental. It is a quiet, grounded environment where you can think clearly, sleep deeply, and step away from the constant noise of everyday life.

Your stay includes a private, well-appointed room within a thoughtfully designed shared home. Upstairs offers natural light, open views, and a welcoming kitchen and living space. Downstairs brings a more relaxed, tucked-away atmosphere with a fireplace, workout area, and direct access to the outdoors.

Step outside and you are on five acres of evolving farmland. Young fruit trees — apple, peach, fig, and asian pear — are taking root alongside established strawberry and blueberry patches, a mature mulberry, and a classic European pear. The land is growing, changing, and becoming something special, and you are part of that moment in time.

Just beyond your door, the Edge of Liberty hosts 13 craft fairs and 13 farm stand days each season. That means a built-in rhythm of optional activity — browse vendors, meet local makers, or simply enjoy the energy — all without ever getting in your car.

And when you need to disappear for a while, there is a tucked-away hidey hole in progress — a quiet, intentional space designed for reading, thinking, or just being left alone.

This is a place for people who:

• want peace without isolation

• appreciate shared spaces that are respected and cared for

• are in transition, working remotely, or simply need a mental reset

• prefer something real over something polished

If that sounds like you, this is where you want to be.
