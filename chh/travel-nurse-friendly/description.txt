## Travel Nurse Friendly — What Matters Here

If you're working long shifts, switching schedules, and living out of a suitcase for weeks or months at a time — where you stay matters.

Create Happiness House is set up to remove friction from daily life and give you a place where you can actually rest, reset, and function between shifts.

---

## What You’ll Get

✔ Fast, Reliable WiFi
Strong, consistent internet throughout the house for streaming, video calls, and staying connected.

✔ Real Sleep (Even During the Day)
Solid light-blocking window treatments and a quiet rural setting help you get real rest — even on a night-shift schedule.

✔ Fully Furnished, Truly Move-In Ready
This is not "bare minimum furnished." Beds, linens, towels, kitchen gear, and daily-use items are already here so you can walk in and live.
Each room also includes its own mini fridge for drinks, snacks, medications, or anything you’d prefer to keep separate from shared spaces.

✔ Laundry That Actually Works
Full washer and dryer on site — shared only within the house, not coin-op, not an afterthought. A dedicated laundry space with folding area and hanging space so you can keep up with real life.

✔ A Desk You’ll Actually Want to Use
Every room has a versatile desk setup that works as both a workspace and a vanity — designed to be practical, comfortable, and actually enjoyable to use.

✔ Kitchen Set Up for Real Cooking
Fully stocked kitchen with cookware, bakeware, utensils, and meal prep containers — not just a pan and a plate. 
There’s also a coffee/tea/wine bar right by the deck for early mornings or late-night decompression, plus a dining table that seats six.

✔ Cleaning Setup That Makes Sense
Common areas are cleaned weekly. Your room is your space — we don’t enter it. You’ll have a vacuum, all-purpose cleaner, and an extra set of sheets so you can keep your space how you like it.

✔ Your Own TV, Your Own Space
Each bedroom has its own TV for your personal use. There are no TVs in shared spaces — no fighting for control, no background noise when you’re trying to rest.

✔ Parking Included
Easy, on-site parking — no hassle after a long shift.

✔ Space to Move
A treadmill and stationary bike are available for convenient workouts at home. You’ll also have access to roughly two miles of walking trails on the property — a quiet way to clear your head after a long shift.
