Is Create Happiness House a Good Fit for Travel Nurses?

We designed this space with real-world needs in mind — especially for professionals working demanding schedules and long shifts.

Reliable internet throughout the home for charting, streaming, and staying connected.

Comfortable, Restful Bedrooms

Firm memory foam queen beds with outlets and lighting built into the headboard.

Designed for Sleep (Even Day Sleep)

Quiet environment with minimal outside noise — ideal for night shift schedules.

Fully Furnished, Move-In Ready

Laundry On Site

Washer and dryer available — no extra cost, no hassle.

Workspace in Every Room

Each room includes a desk that can double as a workspace or vanity.

Storage That Actually Works

Closets with hangers, shelving, and laundry baskets so you can settle in quickly.

Shared Spaces That Respect Privacy

A True Reset Space

From the outdoor setting to the upcoming “hidey hole” retreat space, this is a place designed to help you recharge.
