Danielle Reid Crochet

Handmade crochet and sewn accessories for everyday life — find Danielle Reid Crochet at The Edge of Liberty Craft Fair in Valparaiso, IN (Northwest Indiana).

I sell crochet items mostly market bags, keychains, wristlets, dishcloths, coasters, car coasters, cup coozies, bookmarks, bucket hats, and scrubbies. I also sew hanging dish towels and some tote bags and zipper pouches.
