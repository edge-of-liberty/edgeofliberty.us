Food Trucks at The Edge of Liberty Craft Fair

Food trucks help turn craft fair Sundays into a full afternoon out. Use this page to see which trucks are scheduled for each 2026 date, and which dates are still open for booking.

Event time: Select Sundays, 10 AM - 3 PM at 606 N Calumet Ave, Valparaiso, Indiana. Free admission and parking for visitors.

Food truck note: We are a working farm site, so food trucks need to be self-contained. We do not provide power or potable water.
