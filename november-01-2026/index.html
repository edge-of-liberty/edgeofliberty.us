November 01, 2026

The Edge of Liberty Craft Fair is a lively outdoor market held every other Sunday on the edge of Liberty Township, just north of Valparaiso. Wander through a vibrant mix of handmade goods, unique art, and farm-fresh products from talented local artisans. Enjoy garden walks, explore nature trails, visit the fish pond and apiary, and savor delicious bites from our food truck. With great shopping, live music, and a welcoming, family-friendly atmosphere, it’s the perfect way to spend your Sunday. Free to attend—come experience it all!

Hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM (Central)

Location: 606 N Calumet Ave, Valparaiso, IN 46383
