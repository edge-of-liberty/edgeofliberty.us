ReBloomed

Repurposed glass Forever Flowers & handmade trinkets — find ReBloomed at The Edge of Liberty Craft Fair in Valparaiso, IN (Northwest Indiana).

At ReBloomed, almost everything we make starts with repurposed glass given a second life. Our signature pieces are beaded glass plants we call Forever Flowers — blooms that never wilt. We also make glass trinket trays, resin keychains, and the occasional one-of-a-kind find like a snowman or a skull. When time allows, we create yard art too: flowers made from glass plates mounted on stems you stick right in the ground. Every piece is handmade, and no two are exactly alike.
